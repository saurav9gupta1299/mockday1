Qus 5---Difference between block and inline? 

        A block element always begine in new line and extends to left and right and it take full width of 
         the screen or webpage. Browser automatically add some apace (margin) before and after the element.
        Examples: div, h1 to h6, p, table, form etc.
        
        An inline element does not begin on a new line, it takes only the space that is required or necessary for it. 
        Example : span, anchor tag, img, input etc.
